The Grand Historian says: The enlightened kings of high antiquity who performed music did not do so to entertain their hearts and take joy for themselves, to satisfy their intentions and indulge their desiresâ€”they wished to govern. Correct teachings all begin with sound; when sound is correct, conduct is correct. Therefore, music serves to move and shake the blood vessels, connect and flow the spirit, and harmonize and correct the heart. Therefore, gong moves the spleen and harmonizes and corrects sagehood; shang moves the lung and harmonizes and corrects righteousness; jue moves the liver and harmonizes and corrects benevolence; zhi moves the heart and harmonizes and corrects ritual; yu moves the kidney and harmonizes and corrects wisdom. Therefore, music serves to internally aid in correcting the heart and externally distinguish noble and base; Above, to serve the ancestral temples; below, to transform the common people.
