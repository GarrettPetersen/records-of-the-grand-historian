All your servant's words, not to seek avoid blame, will remonstrate and die, wish Your Majesty for myriad people think follow Way.' Envoy said: 'Your servant received decree execute law on general, dare not with general words report to above.' Meng Tian sighed deeply said: 'I what crime against Heaven, without error die?' Very long time, slowly said: 'Tian crime indeed should die.' Started Lintao connected to Liaodong, walled trenched ten thousand more li, this among cannot without cut earth veins? This is Tian's crime.' Then swallowed medicine suicide.
