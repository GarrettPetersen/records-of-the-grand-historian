Tian Dan was a distant relative of the ruling Tian clan of Qi. During King Min's reign, Tian Dan served as market supervisor in Linzi, where he went unrecognized. When Yan sent Le Yi to attack and defeat Qi, King Min of Qi fled, and afterward defended the city of Ju. The Yan army drove straight ahead to pacify Qi, while Tian Dan fled to Anping and ordered his clansmen to cut off the ends of their chariot axles and fit them with iron casings. Soon the Yan army attacked Anping. The city fell, and the people of Qi fled, crowding the roads. Their chariot axles broke, and they were captured by Yan. Only Tian Dan's clansmen escaped thanks to their iron casings, and they went east to defend Jimo. Yan had completely subdued all the cities of Qi, with only Ju and Jimo holding out. The Yan army heard that the King of Qi was in Ju and concentrated their forces to attack it. Zhao Chi having killed King Min in Ju, thus firmly defended, resisted Yan army, several years not fallen. Yan led troops east to besiege Jimu. Jimu officials went out to battle, defeated and died. City people together promoted Tian Dan, saying: 'In Anping battle, Tian Dan's clansmen with iron casings survived, practiced in arms.' Established him as general, to defend Jimu against Yan.

Yue Yi thus went to Zhao, Yan officers and soldiers resentful. Tian Dan then ordered city people when eating must sacrifice to ancestors in courtyards, flying birds all soared and danced eating in city. Yan people considered it strange. Tian Dan thus declared: 'Spirit comes down to teach me.' Then ordered city people: 'There should be a spirit person to be my teacher.' One soldier said: 'Can I be the teacher?' Thus ran backward. Tian Dan rose, led him back, faced east sat, treated him as teacher. Soldier said: 'I deceived you, truly incapable.' Tian Dan said: 'Do not speak!' Thus treated him as teacher. Whenever going out to give orders, must call divine teacher. Then declared: 'I only fear Yan army will cut noses of captured Qi soldiers, place them in front, battle with me, then Jimu defeated.' Yan heard this, did as said. City people saw all surrendered Qi with cut noses, all angered, firmly defended, feared being captured. Dan again spread false rumors: 'I fear Yan people dig my city outer tombs, dishonor ancestors, can chill the heart.' Yan army completely dug up mounds and tombs, burned corpses. Jimu people from city walls saw, all wept, all wished to go out battle, anger increased tenfold.
