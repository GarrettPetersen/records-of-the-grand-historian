Guan Zhong, whose courtesy name was Yi Wu, was a man of Ying Shang. In his youth he often traveled with Bao Shu Ya, Bao Shu knew his worth. Guan Zhong was impoverished, often deceived Bao Shu, Bao Shu in the end treated him well, did not take it amiss. Soon after Bao Shu served Qi prince Xiao Bai, Guan Zhong served prince Jiu. When Xiao Bai established as Duke Huan, prince Jiu died, Guan Zhong was imprisoned. Bao Shu then recommended Guan Zhong. Guan Zhong having been employed, took charge of government in Qi, Duke Huan of Qi became hegemon, nine times assembled the feudal lords, once set the world in order, it was Guan Zhong's strategy.

Guan Zhong said: 'When I first was in difficulty, I once traded with Bao Shu, divided profits took much for myself, Bao Shu did not consider me greedy, knew I was poor. I once planned affairs for Bao Shu and became even more impoverished, Bao Shu did not consider me foolish, knew there are times of advantage and disadvantage. I once served three times and three times was dismissed by lords, Bao Shu did not consider me unworthy, knew I did not meet the times. I once fought three times and fled three times, Bao Shu did not consider me cowardly, knew I had an aged mother. Prince Jiu was defeated, Zhao Hu died for him, I was imprisoned and humiliated, Bao Shu did not consider me shameless, knew I was not ashamed of small humiliations but ashamed that merit and fame would not be displayed in the world.' Those who gave birth to me are my parents, the one who knows me is Bao Zi.'

His descendants received hereditary stipends in Qi, those with fiefs numbered over ten generations, always were famous grand officers. The world does not praise Guan Zhong's worthiness so much as praises Bao Shu's ability to recognize people.

Guan Zhong having taken charge of government as prime minister of Qi, with tiny Qi by the seacoast, circulated wealth accumulated riches, enriched the state strengthened the army, shared likes and dislikes with the customs. Therefore his saying: 'Granaries full then know ritual and propriety, clothing and food sufficient then know honor and shame, superiors observe standards then the six relatives are secure. The four cardinal principles not upheld, the state then perishes. Issue orders like the source of flowing water, orders follow the people's hearts.' Therefore the principles are lowly and easy to implement. What the customs desire, therefore grant it; what the customs reject, therefore remove it.

In his governance, he was good at turning calamity into fortune, converting defeat into success. Valued balancing light and heavy, was careful with weights and measures. Duke Huan truly angry at Shao Ji, south attacked Cai, Guan Zhong therefore attacked Chu, blamed them for not contributing bundled thatch to the Zhou court. Duke Huan truly north campaigned against the Shan Rong, yet Guan Zhong therefore commanded Yan to restore Duke Shao's governance. At the Kui meeting, Duke Huan wished to renege on Cao Mo's agreement, Guan Zhong therefore kept faith with it, the feudal lords therefore turned to Qi. Therefore said: 'Knowing that giving is taking, is the treasure of governance.'

Yan Ping Zhong Ying was a man of Yi Wei in Lai. Served Duke Ling, Duke Zhuang, Duke Jing of Qi, with frugality and diligent conduct was honored in Qi. Having become prime minister of Qi, did not eat meat twice, concubines did not wear silk. When in court, if the lord's words touched on it, then spoke dangerously; if words did not touch on it, then acted dangerously. If the state had the way, then obeyed commands; if without the way, then balanced commands. With this made famous name among the feudal lords for three generations.

Yue Shi Fu was worthy, in fetters and handcuffs. Yan Zi went out, encountered him on the road, unyoked the left trace horse to redeem him, carried him home. Did not thank him, entered the inner chambers. After a long time, Yue Shi Fu requested to break off relations. Yan Zi fearfully adjusted his clothing and cap apologized saying: 'Ying though unbenevolent, saved you from hempen bonds, why do you seek to break off so quickly?' Shi Fu said: 'Not so. I have heard that gentlemen bend to those who do not know them but extend faith to those who know them. When I was in fetters and handcuffs, they did not know me. When Yan Zi was prime minister of Qi, going out, his charioteer's wife peeked at her husband from the gate. Her husband drove the prime minister's chariot, held the large canopy, whipped the four horses, spirit and energy soaring very self-satisfied. Having returned home, his wife requested to leave. The husband asked the reason. The wife said: 'Yan Zi height not full six feet, personally prime minister of Qi state, name illustrious among feudal lords. Now when I observed his going out, his thoughts and reflections were deep, constantly had ways to humble himself. Now you are eight feet tall, yet serve as another's charioteer, yet your intentions consider yourself sufficient, therefore I request to leave.' Afterwards the husband humbled himself. Yan Zi was surprised and asked him, the charioteer told the truth. Yan Zi recommended him as grand officer.
