Sima Rangju was Tian Wan's descendant. In Qi Jing Gong's time, Jin attacked A and Zhen, while Yan invaded the river region, Qi army suffered defeat. Jing Gong worried about it. Yan Ying then recommended Tian Rangju saying: 'Rangju though Tian clan's bastard branch, yet his civil ability attaches the masses, martial ability awes enemies, wish Your Majesty test him.' Jing Gong summoned Rangju, spoke with him about military affairs, greatly pleased, made him general, led troops to defend against Yan and Jin armies. Rangju said: 'Subject originally lowly, Your Majesty raised me from the ranks, placed me above grand officers, soldiers not yet attached, people not yet trusting, person lowly authority light, wish to obtain Your Majesty's favored minister, the state's honored one, to supervise the army, then acceptable.' Thus Jing Gong permitted it, sent Zhuang Jia to go. Rangju having taken leave, made agreement with Zhuang Jia saying: 'Tomorrow noon meet at army gate.' Rangju first galloped to army, erected pole set water clock awaited Jia. Jia originally arrogant noble, considered the general his own army yet himself the supervisor, not very urgent; relatives and attendants sent him, stayed drinking. At noon Jia not arrived. Rangju then overturned pole broke water clock, entered, conducted army restrained troops, explained regulations. Regulations established, evening time, Zhuang Jia then arrived. Rangju said: 'Why late?' Jia apologized saying: 'Unworthy grand officer's relatives sent me, thus stayed.' Rangju said: 'General receiving command day then forgets his home, facing army regulations then forgets his relatives, grasping drumsticks' urgency then forgets his body. Now enemy states deeply invade, state within disturbed, soldiers exposed on border, lord sleeps not at ease on mat, eats not sweet flavor, people's lives all hang on lord, what called sending off!' Summoned army officer asked saying: 'Military law arriving after appointed time what?' Answered: 'Should behead.' Zhuang Jia feared, sent men galloped to report Jing Gong, requested rescue. Already gone, not yet returned, thus then beheaded Zhuang Jia to show three armies. Three armies' soldiers all trembled. After long time, Jing Gong sent envoy holding tally to pardon Jia, galloped into army. Rangju said: 'General in army, lord's commands have some not accepted.' Asked army officer: 'Galloping through three armies law what?' Officer said: 'Should behead.' Envoy greatly feared. Rangju said: 'Lord's envoy cannot kill him.' Thus beheaded his groom, chariot's left driver, horse's left trace, to show three armies. Sent envoy back to report, then proceeded. Soldiers' encampments wells stoves food drink asked illness medicine, personally stroked and comforted them. All took general's provisions to feast soldiers, body shared rations equally with soldiers. First ranked their weak ones, three days then restrained troops. Sick ones all sought to go, contended to rouse out for battle. Jin army heard it, withdrew and left. Yan army heard it, crossed water and dispersed. Thus pursued them, then took the lost sealed territories former borders and led troops back. Not yet reached state, released troops, dissolved regulations, swore covenant then entered towns. Jing Gong with various grand officers welcomed in suburbs, rewarded army completed rites, then returned to palace. Having seen Rangju, honored him as Grand Marshal. Tian clan day by day increasingly honored in Qi.

Tian Qi, Tian Bao and followers thus resented Gao, Guo and others. Afterwards reached Tian Chang kill Jian Gong, completely extinguished Gao clan, Guo clan lineages. To Chang's great-grandson He, thus self-established as Qi Wei Wang, used troops exercised authority, greatly promulgated Rangju's methods, and feudal lords came to court Qi.
