Xiang Liang crossed the Huai and went west, attacking Jing Ju, Qin Jia, and others. Bu constantly led the vanguard. Xiang Liang arrived at Xue. The Chu troops were constantly victorious, their merit surpassing all the feudal lords. The troops of the feudal lords all thereby submitted and belonged to Chu, because Bu repeatedly defeated many with few.
