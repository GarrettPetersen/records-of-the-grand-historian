In the eighth month, he appointed Tao Qing of Kaifeng, the Imperial Secretary, as Prime Minister. A comet appeared in the northeast. In autumn, Hengshan was struck by hailstones, some as large as five inches across, piling up two feet deep. The planet Mars moved in retrograde and remained near the North Star. The moon appeared between the stars of the Northern Dipper. The planet Jupiter moved in retrograde through the center of the celestial court. The Grand Historian remarks: When the Han dynasty arose, Emperor Xiaowen practiced great benevolence, bringing peace and contentment to the empire. By Emperor Xiaojing's time, there was no longer concern about rulers of different surnames.
