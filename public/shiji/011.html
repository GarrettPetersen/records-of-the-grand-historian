孝景本紀

Annals of the Xiaojing Emperor

1
孝景皇帝者，孝文之中子也。 母竇太后。 孝文在代時，前后有三男，及竇太后得幸，前后死，及三子更死，故孝景得立。
Emperor Xiaojing was the middle son of Emperor Xiaowen. His mother was Empress Dowager Dou. While Emperor Xiaowen was still in Dai as prince, he had three sons from earlier and later periods. After Empress Dowager Dou rose to power, the previous sons died, and then those three sons died as well. This is why Emperor Xiaojing was able to ascend the throne.
2
元年四月乙卯，赦天下。 乙巳，賜民爵一級。 五月，除田半租，為孝文立太宗廟。 令群臣無朝賀。 匈奴入代，與約和親。
In the fourth month of his first year, on the yimao day, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire. On the yisi day, he granted the common people one level of nobility rank. In the fifth month, he eliminated the half-tax on agricultural land and established the Supreme Ancestral Temple for Emperor Xiaowen. He ordered the assembled ministers to dispense with the court congratulations ceremony. The Xiongnu invaded Dai, so he made a treaty of peaceful marriage alliance with them.
3
二年春，封故相國蕭何孫系為武陵侯。 男子二十而得傅。 四月壬午，孝文太后崩。 廣川、長沙王皆之國。 丞相申屠嘉卒。 八月，以御史大夫開封陶青為丞相。 彗星出東北。 秋，衡山雨雹，大者五寸，深者二尺。 熒惑逆行，守北辰。 月出北辰閒。 歲星逆行天廷中。 置南陵及內史、祋祤為縣。
In the spring of his second year, he enfeoffed Xi, grandson of the former Chancellor Xiao He, as Marquis of Wuling. Men at twenty received their registration for military service. On the renwu day of the fourth month, Empress Dowager Xiaowen passed away. The Kings of Guangchuan and Changsha both returned to their fiefs. Chancellor Shen Tu Jia passed away. In the eighth month, he appointed Tao Qing of Kaifeng, the Grandee Secretary, as Chancellor. A comet appeared in the northeast. In autumn, Mount Heng was struck by hail, with the largest stones five inches across and accumulations two feet deep. Mars moved in retrograde motion and remained close to the North Star. The moon appeared close to the North Star. Jupiter moved in retrograde motion within the central region of the Heavenly Court constellation. He established Nanling and also made Neishi and Qirong into counties.
4
三年正月乙巳，赦天下。 長星出西方。 天火燔雒陽東宮大殿城室。 吳王濞、楚王戊、趙王遂、膠西王卬、濟南王辟光、菑川王賢、膠東王雄渠反，發兵西鄉。 天子為誅晁錯，遣袁盎諭告，不止，遂西圍梁。 上乃遣大將軍竇嬰、太尉周亞夫將兵誅之。 六月乙亥。 赦亡軍及楚元王子藝等與謀反者。 封大將軍竇嬰為魏其侯。 立楚元王子平陸侯禮為楚王。 立皇子端為膠西王，子勝為中山王。 徙濟北王志為菑川王，淮陽王餘為魯王，汝南王非為江都王。 齊王將廬、燕王嘉皆薨。
In the first month of his third year, on the yisi day, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire. A comet appeared in the west. Heavenly fire burned the great hall and city chambers of the Eastern Palace in Luoyang. Kings Bi of Wu, Wu of Chu, Sui of Zhao, Ang of Jiaoxi, Pi Guang of Jinan, Xian of Zichuan, and Xiong Qu of Jiaodong rebelled and sent the troops marching westward. The emperor executed Chao Cuo and sent Yuan Ang to admonish the rebels, but they did not stop, so they marched west and besieged Liang. The emperor then sent Grand General Dou Ying and Grand Commandant Zhou Yafu to lead troops and suppress the rebellion. Sixth month, yihai day. He granted amnesty to the deserters and to Prince Yi of Chu and others who had participated in the rebellion. He enfeoffed Grand General Dou Ying as the Marquis of Weiqi. He installed Li, son of the former King Yuan of Chu and Marquis of Pinglu, as the King of Chu. He installed his son Duan as the King of Jiaoxi and his son Sheng as the King of Zhongshan. He transferred King Zhi of Jibei to become the King of Zichuan, King Yu of Huaiyang to become the King of Lu, and King Fei of Runan to become the King of Jiangdu. King Jianglu of Qi and King Jia of Yan both passed away.
5
四年夏，立太子。 立皇子徹為膠東王。 六月甲戌，赦天下。 後九月，更以（弋）［易］陽為陽陵。 復置津關，用傳出入。 冬，以趙國為邯鄲郡。
In the summer of his fourth year, he established the crown prince. He installed his son Che as King of Jiaodong. On the jiaxu day of the sixth month, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire. In the ninth month thereafter, he changed the name of Yiyang to Yangling. He reestablished the ferry checkpoints and required travel tallies for entry and exit. In winter, he converted the state of Zhao into Handan commandery.
6
五年三月，作陽陵、渭橋。 五月，募徙陽陵，予錢二十萬。 江都大暴風從西方來，壞城十二丈。 丁卯，封長公主子蟜為隆慮侯。 徙廣川王為趙王。
In the third month of his fifth year, he constructed Yangling tomb and the Wei Bridge. In the fifth month, he recruited people to relocate to Yangling and gave them 200,000 cash each. A great gale wind came from the west at Jiangdu and damaged twelve zhang of the city wall. On the dingmao day, he enfeoffed Mang, son of the eldest princess, as Marquis of Longlu. He transferred the King of Guangchuan to become King of Zhao.
7
六年春，封中尉（趙）綰為建陵侯，江都丞相嘉為建平侯，隴西太守渾邪為平曲侯，趙丞相嘉為江陵侯，故將軍布為鄃侯。 梁楚二王皆薨。 後九月，伐馳道樹，殖蘭池。
In the spring of his sixth year, he enfeoffed Wan the Commandant-in-Chief as Marquis of Jianling, Jia the Chancellor of Jiangdu as Marquis of Jianping, Hunye the Governor of Longxi as Marquis of Pingqu, Jia the Chancellor of Zhao as Marquis of Jiangling, and Bu the former general as Marquis of Zhu. The Kings of Liang and Chu both passed away. In the ninth month thereafter, he had trees cut down along the imperial highways and planted around the Orchid Pond.
8
七年冬，廢栗太子為臨江王。 （一）十（二）［一］月晦，日有食之。 春，免徒隸作陽陵者。 丞相青免。 二月乙巳，以太尉條侯周亞夫為丞相。 四月乙巳，立膠東王太后為皇后。 丁巳，立膠東王為太子。 名徹。
In the winter of his seventh year, he deposed Crown Prince Li and made him King of Linjiang. On the last day of the eleventh month, there was a solar eclipse. In spring, he exempted convicts and bondservants from working on the Yangling tomb. Chancellor Qing was dismissed from office. On the yisi day of the second month, he appointed Zhou Yafu, Grand Commandant and Marquis of Tiaohou, as Chancellor. On the yisi day of the fourth month, he established the mother of the King of Jiaodong as Empress. On the dingsi day, he established the King of Jiaodong as crown prince. His name was Che.
9
中元年，封故御史大夫周苛孫平為繩侯，故御史大夫周昌（子）［孫］左車為安陽侯，四月乙巳，赦天下，賜爵一級。 除禁錮。 地動。 衡山、原都雨雹，大者尺八寸。
In the first year of the middle period, he enfeoffed Ping, grandson of the former Grandee Secretary Zhou Ke, as Marquis of Sheng, and Zuo Che, grandson of the former Grandee Secretary Zhou Chang, as Marquis of Anyang. On the yisi day of the fourth month, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire and bestowed one level of nobility on all. He lifted all prohibitions and restrictions. There was an earthquake. Mount Heng and Yuandu were struck by hail, with the largest stones measuring one chi and eight cun across.
10
中二年二月，匈奴入燕，遂不和親。 三月，召臨江王來。 即死中尉府中。 夏，立皇子越為廣川王，子寄為膠東王。 封四侯。 九月甲戌，日食。
In the second month of the second year of the middle period, the Xiongnu invaded Yan, and consequently peaceful marriage relations were abandoned. In the third month, he summoned the King of Linjiang to court. He immediately died in the office of the Commandant-in-Chief. In summer, he established his son Yue as the King of Guangchuan and his son Ji as the King of Jiaodong. He enfeoffed four marquises. On the jiaxu day of the ninth month, there was a solar eclipse.
11
中三年冬，罷諸侯御史中丞。 春，匈奴王二人率其徒來降，皆封為列侯。
In the winter of the third year of the middle period, he abolished the position of Grandee Secretary in the feudal states. In spring, two Xiongnu kings led their followers in surrendering, and all were enfeoffed as the marquises.
12
中四年三月，置德陽宮。 大蝗。 秋，赦徒作陽陵者。
In the third month of the fourth year of the middle period, he established the Deyang Palace. There was a great locust plague. In autumn, he granted amnesty to convicts working on the Yangling tomb.
13
中五年夏，立皇子舜為常山王。 封十侯。 六月丁巳，赦天下，賜爵一級。 天下大潦。 更命諸侯丞相曰相。 秋，地動。
In the summer of the fifth year of the middle period, he established his son Shun as King of Changshan. He enfeoffed ten marquises. On the dingsi day of the sixth month, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire and bestowed one level of nobility on all. There were great floods throughout the empire. He changed the title of the feudal lords' chancellors to simply 'chancellor'. In autumn, there was an earthquake.
14
中六年二月己卯，行幸雍，郊見五帝。 三月，雨雹。 四月，梁孝王、城陽共王、汝南王皆薨。 立梁孝王子明為濟川王，子彭離為濟東王，子定為山陽王，子不識為濟陰王。 梁分為五。 封四侯。 更命廷尉為大理，將作少府為將作大匠，主爵中尉為都尉，長信詹事為長信少府，將行為大長秋，大行為行人，奉常為太常，典客為大行，治粟內史為大農。 以大內為二千石，置左右內官，屬大內。 七月辛亥，日食。 八月，匈奴入上郡。
On the jimao day of the second month of the sixth year of the middle period, he made an imperial progress to Yong and performed suburban sacrifices to the Five Emperors. In the third month, it rained hail. In the fourth month, King Xiaowang of Liang, King Gongwang of Chengyang, and the King of Runan all passed away. He established Ming, son of King Xiaowang of Liang, as the King of Jichuan; Pengli as the King of Jidong; Ding as the King of Shanyang; and Bushi as the King of Jiyin. Liang was divided into five kingdoms. He enfeoffed four marquises. He changed the title of Superintendent of Trials to Grand Judge, Superintendent of the Imperial Workshops to Grand Artisan of the Imperial Workshops, Superintendent of the Nobility to Commandant of the Capital, Director of the Palace of Enduring Trust to Vice Director of the Palace of Enduring Trust, Chamberlain for the Imperial Harem to Grand Chamberlain for the Imperial Harem, Chamberlain for Ceremonials to Master of Protocol, Grand Master of Ceremonies to Grand Master of Ceremonies, Superintendent of Guests to Grand Herald, and Superintendent of the Granaries to Grand Minister of Agriculture. He made the Grand Interior a 2000 shi position, established left and right interior officials, subordinate to the Grand Interior. On the xinhai day of the seventh month, there was a solar eclipse. In the eighth month, the Xiongnu invaded Shangjun commandery.
15
後元年冬，更命中大夫令為衛尉。 三月丁酉，赦天下，賜爵一級，中二千石、諸侯相爵右庶長。 四月，大酺。 五月丙戌，地動，其蚤食時復動。 上庸地動二十二日，壞城垣。 七月乙巳，日食。 丞相劉舍免。 八月壬辰，以御史大夫綰為丞相，封建陵侯。
In the winter of the first year thereafter, he changed the title of Commander of the Palace Gentlemen to Guard Commander. On the dingyou day of the third month, he granted a general amnesty throughout the empire and bestowed one level of nobility on all. Officials of 2000 shi rank and feudal lords' chancellors received the nobility rank of You Shuzhang. In the fourth month, there was a great public celebration with drinking. On the bingxu day of the fifth month, there was an earthquake, and it shook again at breakfast time. In Shangyong, the earth shook for twenty-two days and damaged the city walls. On the yisi day of the seventh month, there was a solar eclipse. Chancellor Liu She was dismissed from office. On the renchen day of the eighth month, he appointed Wan, the Grandee Secretary, as Chancellor and enfeoffed him as the Marquis of Jianling.
16
後二年正月，地一日三動。 郅將軍擊匈奴。 酺五日。 令內史郡不得食馬粟，沒入縣官。 令徒隸衣七緵布。 止馬舂。 為歲不登，禁天下食不造歲。 省列侯遣之國。 三月，匈奴入雁門。 十月，租長陵田。 大旱。 衡山國、河東、雲中郡民疫。
In the first month of the second year thereafter, the earth shook three times in one day. General Zhi attacked the Xiongnu. There was a public drinking celebration for five days. He ordered that the Interior commanderies should not feed grain to horses, and such grain should be confiscated for government use. He ordered that convicts and bondservants should wear clothing made of seven-span cloth. He stopped the practice of using horses to tread grain. Because the harvest was poor, he prohibited people throughout the empire from eating grain that was not from the current year's harvest. He reduced the number of marquises and sent them back to their fiefs. In the third month, the Xiongnu invaded Yanmen commandery. In the tenth month, he leased out the fields of the Changling tomb. There was a great drought. There was an epidemic among the people of Hengshan state and the Hedong and Yunzhong commanderies.
17
後三年十月，日月皆（食）赤五日。 十二月晦，雷。 日如紫。 五星逆行守太微。 月貫天廷中。 正月甲寅，皇太子冠。 甲子，孝景皇帝崩。 遺詔賜諸侯王以下至民為父後爵一級，天下戶百錢。 出宮人歸其家，復無所與。 太子即位，是為孝武皇帝。 三月，封皇太后弟蚡為武安侯，弟勝為周陽侯。 置陽陵。
In the tenth month of the third year thereafter, both the sun and moon appeared red for five days. There was thunder on the last day of the twelfth month. The sun appeared purple. The five planets moved in retrograde motion and remained close to the Taiwei constellation. The moon passed through the center of the Heavenly Court constellation. On the jiayin day of the first month, the crown prince came of age. On the jiachen day, Emperor Xiaojing passed away. In his testament, he granted one level of nobility to all feudal kings and below, down to commoners who succeeded their fathers, and gave one hundred cash to every household throughout the empire. He released the palace women to return to their homes and exempted them from all taxes and labor obligations. The crown prince ascended the throne; this was Emperor Xiaowu. In the third month, he enfeoffed the Empress Dowager's younger brother Tian Fen as the Marquis of Wuan and her younger brother Tian Sheng as the Marquis of Zhouyang. He established the Yangling tomb.
18
太史公曰：漢興，孝文施大德，天下懷安，至孝景，不復憂異姓，而晁錯刻削諸侯，遂使七國俱起，合從而西鄉，以諸侯太盛，而錯為之不以漸也。 及主父偃言之，而諸侯以弱，卒以安。 安危之機，豈不以謀哉？
The Grand Historian says: When the Han arose, Emperor Xiaowen practiced great virtue, and all under Heaven lived in peace. By Emperor Xiaojing's time, there was no longer concern about rulers of different surnames. But Chao Cuo's harsh reduction of the feudal lords' power caused all seven states to rise up, form an alliance, and march westward. This happened because the feudal lords had grown too powerful, and Cuo acted without proper gradual measures. When Zhu Fu Yan spoke out about this situation, the feudal lords were thereby weakened, and finally there was peace. The pivot between security and peril—is it not through strategic planning?
