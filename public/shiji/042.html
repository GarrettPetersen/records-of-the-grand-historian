Duke Huan of Zheng, whose personal name was You, was the youngest son of King Li of Zhou and the half-brother of King Xuan. In the twenty-second year of King Xuan's reign, You was first enfeoffed with the territory of Zheng. He held the fief for thirty-three years, during which all the people found him competent and loved him. King You appointed him Minister of Works. He harmonized and united the Zhou people. The Zhou people were all pleased with him. Between the Yellow and Luo Rivers, people fondly remembered him. He served as Minister of Works for one year. King You, because of the affair with Lady Bao, allowed much corruption in the royal court. Some of the feudal lords rebelled against him. Thereupon Huan Gong asked Tai Shi Bo: 'The wang chamber has many troubles. Where can I escape death?' Tai Shi Bo replied: 'Only the land east of Luo, south of the He and Ji rivers can be resided in.' The gong said: 'Why?' He replied: 'The land is near Guo and Xu. The lords of Guo and Xu are greedy and fond of profit. The common people do not attach to them. Now you are Minister of Works. The people all love you. If you sincerely request to reside there, the lords of Guo and Xu, seeing that you are just coming into power, will readily grant you land. If the gong sincerely resides there, the people of Guo and Xu will all be the gong's people.' The gong said: 'I desire to go south to Jiang Shang. What do you think?' He replied: 'Formerly Zhu Rong was Gao Xin Shi's fire official. His merit was great, but he did not yet have prosperity in Zhou. Chu is his descendant. When Zhou declines, Chu will surely rise. Prospering there would not be to Zheng's profit.' The gong said: 'I desire to reside in the west. What do you think?' He replied: 'Their people are greedy and fond of profit. It is difficult to reside there long.' The gong said: 'When Zhou declines, which states will prosper?' He replied: 'Qi, Qin, Jin, and Chu?' Now Qi, of the Jiang clan, is descended from Bo Yi. Bo Yi assisted Yao in overseeing the rituals. Qin, of the Ying clan, is descended from Bo Yi. Bo Yi assisted Shun in pacifying and nurturing all things. And Chu's ancestors all once rendered great service to the world. But after Zhou Wu Wang conquered Zhou, Cheng Wang enfeoffed Shu Yu at Tang. His land was obstructed and dangerous. With this virtue alongside Zhou's decline, he will certainly prosper.' Duke Huan said: 'Excellent.' Thereupon he finally spoke to the king and moved his people east of Luoyang. Guo and Xu indeed granted him ten cities, completing the establishment of the state.
