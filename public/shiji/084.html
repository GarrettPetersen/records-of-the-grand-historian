Qu Ping resented that the king listened without wisdom, that slanderers blocked clarity, that crookedness harmed justice, that uprightness was not tolerated. Its text is concise, its diction subtle, its ambition pure, its conduct honest. Its language is modest but its meaning extremely grand, citing near examples yet revealing far-reaching principles. Its ambition pure, thus it praises fragrant things. Its conduct honest, thus in death it would not let itself be distant.
