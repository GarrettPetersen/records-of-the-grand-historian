1
制詔御史：「諸侯王或欲推私恩分子弟邑者，令各條上，朕且臨定其號名。」
Imperial Edict to the Imperial Secretary: 'When nobles and kings wish to bestow private favor by dividing fiefs to younger brothers and sons, let each one memorialize separately. I will personally determine their titles and names.' (translated).
2
太史公曰：盛哉，天子之德！ 一人有慶，天下賴之。
The Grand Historian said: How flourishing is the virtue of the Son of Heaven! (translated). When one person has good fortune, the entire empire relies upon it.
