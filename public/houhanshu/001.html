The Book of the Later Han is a biographical history that records the history of the Eastern Han Dynasty. The work is divided into ten annals, eighty biographies, and eight treatises. (The latter part was continued by Sima Biao) . The author of the annals and biographies was Fan Ye of the Liu Song Dynasty during the Southern Dynasties period. During the reign of Emperor Zhenzong of the Northern Song, the eight treatises in thirty volumes from Sima Biao's Continued Book of Han (dating from the Jin Dynasty) were combined and published with Fan Ye's work, forming the Book of the Later Han as it exists today.
