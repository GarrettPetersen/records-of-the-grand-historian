Emperor Zhao the Filial was Emperor Wu's youngest son. His mother was Consort Zhao, who originally gained the emperor's favor due to her extraordinary qualities; when she gave birth to the emperor, it was also extraordinary. The full account is in the Biography of the Imperial Relatives by Marriage. The Emperor was five years old when Emperor Wu passed away. On the day Wuyin, the crown prince ascended the imperial throne and paid respects at the Gao Temple. He honored the Empress Dowager as Grand Empress Dowager, and the Empress as Empress Dowager. Summer, sixth month, granted amnesty to the world. Autumn, seventh month, day Gengshen, enfeoffed the Empress's father Marquis Fengguang Hao as Right Prefect of Fufeng, with his marquisate as before. Ninth month, edict said: 'I, with my insignificant person, have obtained the protection of the ancestral temples; I am fearful and cautious, afraid of not being worthy.
