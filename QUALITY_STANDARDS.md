# Quality Standards Reminder

## Ken Liu Translation Quality Requirements

### Core Principles:
- **Semantic fidelity**: Accurate meaning preservation
- **Modern readability**: Natural, flowing English
- **No added narrative**: Stick to source text meaning
- **No stylistic ornament**: Clean, direct translation

### Technical Requirements:
- Both literal AND idiomatic translations required
- Literal: Word-for-word accuracy
- Idiomatic: Natural English flow while preserving meaning
- No placeholders: (translated), TODO, ..., etc.
- Complete sentences with proper grammar
- Reasonable length compared to Chinese text

### Quality Checks (Automated):
- Length ratio validation (not too short)
- Placeholder text detection
- Article/grammar warnings
- Identical translation prevention
- Chinese character contamination detection
